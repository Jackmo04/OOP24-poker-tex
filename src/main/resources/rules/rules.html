How to Play

The Basics

A game is composed of a series of hands. Each hand progresses as follows:

Two cards are dealt face down to each player, and then a total of five community cards are dealt face up during the four phases of the game. (see Game Phases)

Each player seeks the best poker combination obtainable from the seven cards at their disposal (the five community cards plus their two personal cards). (see Combinations)

During each phase the players are allowed to make bets which will be added to a pot. The player who has the best combination at the end of the hand, or is the only player who hasn't folded, wins the pot. (see Betting)
